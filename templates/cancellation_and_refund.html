Cancellation & Refund

At Witeso.com, we offer various products and services tailored to suit your needs. Once you have purchased credits from us, we do not initiate refunds under any circumstances. This includes but is not limited to changes in your requirements, dissatisfaction with the product, or any other reason.

Exceptions:

Despite our stringent cancellation and refund policy, we understand that there may be exceptional cases. All payments made through Razorpay are encrypted and processed securely, providing you with peace of mind when making purchases on our platform.

If you have any questions or concerns regarding our cancellation and refund policy, please feel free to contact us. Our customer support team is available to assist you and address any inquiries you may have at Contact Page.

Thank you for your understanding and cooperation.
